Utility NFTs

"Utility NFT" refers to any NFT that has real tangible value apart from acting as static digital collectibles. This can include a wide variety of things such as physical gifts associated, special events and other exclusive offers for NFT holders to get excited about. The possibilities are endless and we would love to discuss what utility NFTs could bring to the table for your brand.

Marketing & Community

The most important factor for any NFT launch is hype surrounding the project. We will heavily market your project through to our growing group of passionate followers, and share your project with influential people within the community in order to spread the word. Combining this with your own social media platforms to get your fans engaged is a recipe for success to help ensure your project sells out.

Smart Contracts

Smart contracts provide the logic behind every blockchain application, from NFT to automated market maker (AMM). Our engineering team has extensive experience with EVM-based smart contracts (i.e. those written in Solidity and deployed on Ethereum or Polygon), as well as other blockchains, such as Algorand and NEAR.
